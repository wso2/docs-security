### AFFECTED PRODUCTS
* WSO2 Identity Server: 7.1.0, 7.0.0


### OVERVIEW
Potential information disclosure risk in multi-tenant deployment.


### DESCRIPTION
Due to improper validation, personally identifiable user information may be exposed when using Email OTP, SMS OTP, or Magic Link as first-factor authenticators in multi-tenant deployments. This leads to cross-tenant information disclosure and potential privacy violations.


### IMPACT
Successful exploitation could allow cross-tenant disclosure of personally identifiable information, leading to privacy violations, unauthorized access to user details like mobile number, and potential regulatory non-compliance.


### SOLUTION

#### Community Users (Open Source)
Apply the relevant fixes to your product using the public fix(es) provided below.

* [https://github.com/wso2-extensions/identity-auth-otp-commons/pull/30](https://github.com/wso2-extensions/identity-auth-otp-commons/pull/30)
* [https://github.com/wso2-extensions/identity-local-auth-emailotp/pull/65](https://github.com/wso2-extensions/identity-local-auth-emailotp/pull/65)
* [https://github.com/wso2-extensions/identity-local-auth-magiclink/pull/76](https://github.com/wso2-extensions/identity-local-auth-magiclink/pull/76)
* [https://github.com/wso2/carbon-identity-framework/pull/7496](https://github.com/wso2/carbon-identity-framework/pull/7496)

If applying the fix or update is not feasible, migrate to the latest unaffected version of the respective WSO2 product(s).
